Mission to Mercury

BepiColombo

BepiColombo is a joint mission of the European Space Agency (ESA) and the Japan Aerospace Exploration Agency (JAXA) to the planet Mercury. The mission comprises two satellites launched together: the Mercury Planetary Orbiter (MPO) and Mio (Mercury Magnetospheric Orbiter, MMO). The mission will perform a comprehensive study of Mercury, including its magnetic field, magnetosphere, interior structure and surface. It was launched on 20 October 2018 at 01:45 UTC, with an arrival at Mercury planned for December 2025, after a flyby of Earth, two flybys of Venus, and six flybys of Mercury. The mission was approved in November 2009, after years in proposal and planning as part of the European Space Agency's Horizon 2000+ program; it is the last mission of the program to be launched.

Google Translate predicted the Second Coming

One of the users of the Google Translate service found that when translating the repeatedly written word dog from the language of new Zealand aboriginal Maori into English, the service gives a rather articulate prediction of the Second coming of Jesus Christ. It is reported by the Next Web.

Classical machine translation works through the use of multiple parallel corpora (source and target language texts). This translation is also called "statistical" machine translation, and recently it is also complemented by the use of deep learning that allows you to take into account the context: for example, two years ago, Google made the most accurate machine translation using a neural network, and last autumn Yandex presented its "hybrid" version of the translator.

Both the advantage and disadvantage of modern machine translation systems is that they translate everything: even those words that are not in the language. Of course, the translator informs the user of a source error by asking the user to select a different language; however, sometimes such a translation produces unexpected results.

The same happened with the Maori language, in which there is no word "dog": Google Translate, received the input several times written word, translated it into English whole sentence-a foreshadowing of the Second coming of Jesus Christ and the End of the world:

Doomsday Clock is three minutes at twelve We are experiencing characters and a dramatic developments in the world, which indicate that we are increasingly approaching the end times and Jesus’ return.

According to the Next Web, their Google source reported that the resulting text was meaningless and was merely a reaction of the translation system to a meaningless source fragment. The religious bias of the resulting translation can be explained by the fact that biblical texts are still the most popular and the main source for the creation of parallel corpora, especially rare ones. Last year, German linguists even assembled a corpus consisting of more than a thousand translations of the New Testament.
